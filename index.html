My name is Esteban Terrien, born in 2004, my interest in game was always quite peculiar: I would play until I understand everything, every mechanic, every game design decision to the point of simulating the game in my mind. While this process was really fun, I soon realise I could do better: why not actually make some?

So right after Highschool I went into a Game Design Bachelor, to learn the industry standard and challenge myself with things I would have never done on my own.

Now with the technical skills to back up my idea I aim to engineer ludic environments that tell stories, make people feel things and satisfy my need for complex problem solving.
